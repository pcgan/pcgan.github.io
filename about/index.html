Hello, Welcome to here, a beautiful portfolio of phos and ink

Life could be a painful journey, but the experience is rich and wonderful all the time. If somewhere a few fragments could be inlaid, it must be a fascinating and frightening space. I’ve always been obsessed with strange ideas, caught up by the beauty, and occasionally would like to escape from selfishness and loneliness, so this place was born.

When your “here” has become a “there”, the only thing you can do is to keep yourself from forgetting. When the scene floats in your mind., there is a photograph. When the art of light hits the ocean of thought, the pages would be embedded in your canvas just as a star is litted in night sky, so I hope I could save all what I caught. There are sixpences everywhere, hope you could find the moon here.
